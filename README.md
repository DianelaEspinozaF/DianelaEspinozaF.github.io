## About Me

Hi all! I am Dianela, from Chiclayo, Peru. I am a Master's candidate in Data Science and Public Policy at the London School of Economics (LSE). I have a strong interest in topics related to education, early-childhood development, and impact evaluation.

## Professional Experience

I worked with Santiago Cueto and Alan Sanchez on the international longitudinal study Young Lives, focusing on:
- Conditional cash transfer interventions and their impact on educational outcomes
- Data cleaning processes with high standards

### Research Intern | Innovations for Poverty Action
I contributed to projects including:
- Closing gender gaps in STEM careers in Belize
- Intercultural Bilingual Education interventions during kindergarten
